Information on the digital audio output of the MyHD
---------------------------------------------------

SPDIF Clock Frequencies:

Sample rate	SPDIF Clock
-----------	-----------
32.0kHz		4.0960MHz
44.1kHz		5.6448MHz
48.0kHz		6.1440MHz

The MK2770-01 on the MyHD MDP-110 has an audio clock line, with
selectable frequencies matching 2x the frequencies in the above table.
There is also an exact match for the 5.6448MHz clock.
